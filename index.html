SITE SEIZURE NOTICE

The website https://norbin.vercel.app (operating under the alias "Norbin Leaks") has been permanently seized by federal authorities.

This action is part of Operation "SILENTBREACH," a multi-agency task force targeting illegal information trafficking networks.

CRIMINAL CHARGES

The operators of Norbin have been indicted on the following charges:

  • Conspiracy to Commit Computer Intrusion (18 U.S.C. § 1030)
  • Theft of Government Property (18 U.S.C. § 641)
  • Unauthorized Access to Protected Computers (18 U.S.C. § 1030(a)(2))
  • Aggravated Identity Theft (18 U.S.C. § 1028A)
  • Money Laundering (18 U.S.C. § 1956)
  • Espionage Act Violations (18 U.S.C. § 792-799)
  • RICO Conspiracy (18 U.S.C. § 1962)
